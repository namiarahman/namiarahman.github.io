My Creative Corner

"The Crimson Return"
An abstract meditation on the 'hour of return,' using saturated crimson skies and a still shoreline to capture the communal peace of fishermen finishing their day's labor.

"Vibrant Nights"
A vibrant subversion of 'Starry Night' that reimagines the night as a cheerful, lawless space, swapping the moon for a sun to reflect the electric energy of abstract freedom.

"The Golden Vessel"
A visual meditation on Rabindranath Tagore’s 'Shonar Tori,' using moody teals and indigo waters to capture the moment creation is carried away while the creator is left behind on the rain-swept shore.

"The Quiet Alchemy"
A nostalgic tribute to childhood blackouts, using indigo swirls and a single golden flicker to capture how a candle flame transformed a dark room into a space of spirit and secret languages.

"The Reach"
A visual meditation on the vertical line. Through a combination of palette knife textures and fluid washes, this piece captures the spirit of the voyager, small in stature, but bold in its ascent toward the light.

"Rickshawchitro"
"A celebration of Bangladeshi rickshaw art, using kaleidoscopic motifs of birds and floral patterns to capture the melodramatic beauty and resilient spirit of traditional hand-painted street craft.
